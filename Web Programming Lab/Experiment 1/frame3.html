BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women is just not another Engineering college in Hyderabad. It was established in the year of 2012 with the clear vision & mission to empower the women to aspire for a better career in their life. It was started with an intake of 240 students of four different branches EEE, ECE, CSE and IT. But through consistent academic excellence, the college could extend the intake up to 540 students with New Programs in UG – CSE (AI&ML) and PG – M.Tech (Data Science). The Institute is duly approved by All India Council for Technical Education and the Government of Telangana State and is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad.
